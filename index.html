About Us

Independence Day Event

Punjab Engineering College( Deemed to be University ) celebrated 75th Independence Day with huge Pomp and Joy. And on this great day, PEC Robotics Society added to the flamboyance of this event with their technical display and caused a whim of excitement in audiences. The Robotics team made an eye catching wireless Bluetooth controlled robot through which they served momento to the chief guest and distributed prizes amongst winners. It was a wonderful display of technology. But this wasn't enough, Robotics society also had an amazing drone prepared for flag hoisting ceremony. It was just like cherry on the cake.

  • The Robot was made from an iron chassis built by the students themselves and Arduino was used as the microcontroller in it.
  • Robotics society also had an amazing drone prepared for flag hoisting ceremony.
  • The drone was used to shower petals on the people present in the ceremony.
